Descrption

Potato_salad is a simple and quick recipe that just that just requires simple ingredients,one can have it as a brunch or mid-day food
 It is also a nutrious meal that can be eaten any time of the day especially to those with busy shedules.

ingredients

  • 3 potatoes
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • lettice
  • celery
  • yellow pepper
  • salt
  • 1 bottle mayonise

Steps

  1. Get 3potatoes
  2. Wash them
  3. Peal them
  4. Cuts sized pieces
  5. Put in a small pot,to boil for 15minutes
  6. Leave them to cool
  7. Get 3eggs boil them for 15minutes
  8. Leave to cool,then peal the boiled eggs
  9. cut the eggs into small pieces
  10. Get a small sarving dish put the eggs and cold potatoes
  11. Cut an onion into small pieces
  12. Cut green pepper into small pieces
  13. Cut lettice into small pieces
  14. Add mayonise,
  15. Add a table spoon of yellow pepper powder
  16. Add a pinch on salt
  17. mix all the ingredients
  18. Ganish with celery
  19. Sarve cold with a cold glass of juice.
